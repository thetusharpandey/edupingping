Delivery Policy
Thank you for shopping with us! We aim to get your order to you as quickly and efficiently as possible. Please review the following policy to understand our shipping process. Processing Time All orders are processed within 1–3 business days. Orders are not shipped or delivered on weekends or holidays. If we are experiencing a high volume of orders, shipments may be delayed by a few days. Please allow additional days in transit for delivery. If there will be a significant delay in the shipment of your order, we will contact you via email. Shipping Rates & Delivery Estimates Shipping charges for your order will be calculated and displayed at checkout. Standard Delivery: Delivery typically takes 5–7 business days after processing. Express Delivery: Delivery typically takes 2–3 business days after processing. Delivery delays can occasionally occur. We will not be responsible for any customs and taxes applied to your order. All fees imposed during or after shipping are the responsibility of the customer (tariffs, taxes, etc.). Shipping Confirmation & Order Tracking You will receive a Shipping Confirmation email once your order has shipped, containing your tracking number(s). The tracking number will be active within 24 hours. International Shipping We offer international shipping to most countries. Shipping costs and delivery times for international orders will vary based on the destination. Please be aware that you, the customer, are responsible for any import duties, customs taxes, or fees charged by your country. These charges are not included in the shipping cost.
